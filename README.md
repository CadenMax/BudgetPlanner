# Budget Elite

Open source budget calculator for Australians with variable income. Perfect for casual workers, freelancers, or anyone whose pay does not arrive in predictable, equal chunks.

Built around the 50/30/20 rule, but flexible enough to handle real life.

---

## ATO Tax Integration

Budget Elite automatically calculates PAYG withholding using the official ATO weekly tax tables. No more manual guessing or spreadsheet headaches.

**Reference:** [ATO Weekly tax tables](https://www.ato.gov.au/tax-rates-and-codes/tax-table-weekly)

---

## Key Features

### Custom Categories
Set up your own Wants, Needs, and Savings/Investments line by line. Each item can be a:
- Fixed dollar amount, or
- Percentage of your income (after tax)

### Flexible Pay Periods
Choose between weekly, fortnightly, or monthly schedules. Works seamlessly with irregular hours. Just enter your hours worked and hourly rate each period.

### Freeloader Money
Budget for money you receive but do not actually spend (for example, parental help with rent, gifts, or reimbursements). See what your lifestyle would cost without that help while keeping your personal spending accurate.

### Account Tracking
Split your budget across multiple bank accounts. Know exactly which account gets how much. No more guesswork at transfer time.

### Big Spending Planner
Planning a large purchase this week or soon? Enter the amount, and Budget Elite shows:
- How much extra you would need to save
- How it affects your regular savings or investments

The rest automatically reallocates to savings, keeping your plan intact.

---

## How to Use

1. **Set up your budget items**  
   Add your Wants, Needs, and Savings goals. Choose fixed dollar amounts or percentages per item.

2. **Enter your work details**  
   Hours worked this period plus your hourly pay rate.

3. **Let it calculate**  
   The tool applies ATO tax tables, then distributes your net income according to your rules.

4. **Optional extras**  
   Toggle Freeloader Money, track accounts, or run a Big Spending scenario.
